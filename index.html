Welcome!

Welcome to Mapleville Farm! Nestled in the heart of Mapleville, in the Northwest corner of Rhode Island, we are a small family-owned and operated business. Though we are a very small farm by definition, there are several different facets to our operation, including baked goods, preserves, fresh produce and eggs, and livestock.
