Old-Fashioned Cookies

We don't just make Italian cookies, of course, and so far as family tradition goes, what could be more universal than baking chocolate chip cookies with Grandma (Nana, Granny, Noni, Oma, Mama, Lala, Babushka, Snookums)? These are a few of our enduring favorites: again, almost exclusively from original family recipies handed down through the generations.

Peanut Butter

$2.00 each

Nomm-nomm-nomm. We're huge peanut butter people, and we make our cookies exclusively with the best—Brown Bear of Providence. These are serious cookies. Big, moist and delicious.

Gingerbread Trooper

Seasonal Our own example of the classic holiday favorite is a real bruiser, standing more than 5" and packing a mean fistfulla spice. When Ginger goes for a swim in cold milk, he doesn't get wet; the milk gets Ginger.

Chocolate Chip

$2.00 each

The quintessential cookie. Ours are smooth and buttery and positively loaded with high-quality semi-sweet morsels. Best with a tall glass of cold milk.
