Artisan Breads

Several years ago, Ben, our resident bread scientist, set forth on an epic quest to create the perfect loaf. Although that goal has not yet been realized, he's come so darn close it's scary.

Working the dough by hand, using only the finest natural ingredients, and a unique long fermentation method (to coax every bit of flavor out of the flour), we put our heart and soul (and back) into every loaf. Because all bread should be, and the best bread is, a labor of love.
