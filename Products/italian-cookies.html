Italian Cookies

Thanks in no small part to our Italian heritage, we have a deep passion for good, wholesome food. Most of our Italian goods are original family recipies that have stood the test of time, being passed down through the generations.

All of our cookies are made by hand, with no artificial preservatives and only the freshest ingredients. We specialize in assorted cookie trays, which are perfect for parties, family gatherings, weddings, and corporate and other social events.

Wine Biscuits

60¢ each

Made with a good Burgundy, they're moist and soft when fresh; however, if you like them the traditional way, leave them in the open air for a couple of days, or dry them out in the oven until they're crunchy, and then (you guessed it) soak them in wine.

Butter Cookies

50¢ each

A holiday classic. These simply delicious cookies have become a part of many families' Holiday traditions over the years—a part that kids and parents alike have told us that they look forward to.

Almond Biscotti

$2.50

The Classic Original. We bake all of our biscotti only once (technically that makes them unscotti) because in our opinion they're tastier (and less painful) that way. We will double-bake upon request for you hardcore dunkers out there.

Espresso Biscotti

$2.50

This is the tall, dark and mysterious stranger in our biscotti club. Full-bodied, rich and strong. Don't eat it before bedtime, kids.

Butterballs

65¢ each

Buttery little pecan cookies rolled in powdered sugar. A-mamma-mia! Ever-accomodating, we also sell them plain for those who don't like sugar.

Drizzled Butter Cookies

75¢ each

This is the teaser in the butter cookie family, delicately drizzled with just enough warm, smooth, milk chocolate to make your heart sing and your tastebuds scream with delight.

Pistachio Biscotti

$2.50

We roast our own pistachios for our biscotti, and it shows. For years, this one has been the top contender for the Public Favorite–standing out for its nutty warm flavor.

Chocolate-Hazelnut Biscotti

$2.50

Loaded with dark chocolate chunks and hazelnuts, with delicious notes of Irish cream and caramel.

Prune Cookies

80¢ each

Think prunes are yucky? Think again! For these nummy cookies we cook prunes, walnuts, maraschino and cherries down into a fruity filling, then roll it up in our butter cookie dough and sprinkle with confectioners' sugar for a delightful treat.

Dunked Butter Cookies

$1.00 each

Unlike its sister Drizzle, the Dunked Butter Cookie keeps no secrets about what it wants to be. Completely immersed in a rich dark chocolate, it is deep and smooth and soulful. It pairs particularly well with good coffee.

Cran-Map-Wal Biscotti

$2.50

Combining cranberry, maple, and walnuts, this is all of New England in a single cookie. The sweetest of our biscotti, it is also the most serious challenger to Pistachio's place as Public Favorite.
