Jams & Jellies

All of our jams and jellies are produced in small quantities to ensure quality of product. We use only fresh, locally grown ingredients in our preserves. The response has been overwhelmingly positive; several of our regular customers have sworn off factory produced, brand-name products altogether, saying they could "never go back."

Grape Jelly

$5.50/half-pint
$7.50/pint

Grape jelly was the first preserve we experimented with, several years ago. Since then we've perfected the recipe, which is made with fresh-picked Concords grown entirely on our property or in our village.

Strawberry Jam

$5.50/half-pint
$7.50/pint

Our sweet, jammin' strawberry jam is an all-time favorite. Made with strawberries picked locally at nearby Rhode Island and Massachusetts farms, this spiffy stuff is enough to make your mouth water. We like ours on Italian toast!

Peach Jam

$6.50/half-pint
$8.50/pint

Our peach jam is a sweet ode to New England autumns. Pair it with a freshly baked muffin for a delightful breakfast, or with crackers for a fancy snack. The peaches are picked every year from local orchards for this truly New England treat.
